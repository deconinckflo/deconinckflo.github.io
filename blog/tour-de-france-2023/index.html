Maven Tour de France Challenge

  • Deconinck Florian
  • Thursday, Jul 27, 2023

Introduction

As part of the Maven Tour de France challenge, we have to put ourselves in the shoes of a data visualization expert working for Amaury Sport Organisation, the company that organizes the annual Tour de France cycling race.

The Tour de France is one of the most watched live sporting events in the world, but most viewers come from European countries.

Challenge

So my aim is to create an infographic-style visual to educate new viewers, highlight the scale of the event and build anticipation for this year’s race. This exercise is an opportunity for me to develop my data visualization skills.

Data

Historical dataset on every Tour de France from 1903 to 2022, including details on the stages, distance, entrants, finishers, and winning times for each year.
